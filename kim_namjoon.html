Kim Namjoon Profile

Birth Name: Kim Namjoon
Stage Name: RM formerly known as Rap Monster
Position: Leader of the group, main rapper
Birthday: September 12, 1994
Height: 180 cm (5'11")
Weight:73.6kg (162 lbs)
Blood Type: A
Spotify List: RM's Favorite Tracks
Instagram: @rkive

Kim Namjoon Facts

-Born in Seoul (Sangdo-Dong) then moved to Ilsan, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea when he was 4 years old.
-Has a younger sister named Kim Kyung Min.
-RM studied languages in New Zealand for 4 months in 2006, as mentioned in Bon Voyage 4 - Ep. 1
-His favorite foods are meat based especailly Samgyeopsal and kalguksu which are Korean knife noodles.
-He taught himself English and can speak it very well. He acts as the translater for the group as the members slowly gain more knowledge and do their best to also converse in English whenever they can
-Despite his rough and tough image, he is very playful and relaxed
-His favorite number is 1
-He has co-composed 160+ songs
-He released his first solo mixtape, "RM" on March 17, 2015
-November 13, 2017, he changed his name from Rap Monster to "RM" which he says stands for "Real Me"
